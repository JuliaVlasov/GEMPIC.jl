    GEMPIC.ParticleGroup — Type.
    ParticleGroup{D,V}( n_particles, 
                    charge, 
                    mass, 
                    n_weights)
    • n_particles : number of particles
    • charge : charge of one particle
    • mass : mass of one particle
    • n_weights : number of differents weights
